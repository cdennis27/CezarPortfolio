About me

I always enjoyed technology and cars, since a kid. Lately I had a great time building and restoring classic cars, sometimes with a modern twist in them, trying to keep the classic design looks and the modern parts hidden inside to improve the use of it.

Who doesn't like when the car can drive far away without breaking down, or having the speedometer match the actual speed of the car? Imagine having air conditioning on a classic car in the Californian heat? Classic cars sometimes are a piece of art that stay stationary because they aren't comfortable to drive, but with customization and modernization you can connect both modern comfort to the classic design that is so desired by several people!

From childhood in the 90's self taught about computers and mechanics, designed very basic concepts to more complex concepts modifying cars and equipment for more performance and efficiency. Always enjoying gaming and technological products. Racing cars and manufacturing custom parts and modifying existing components to improve performance.

When a project starts the work begins, there is a lot of planning and research to execution and then the delivery. The process of managing a project is accomplished when product is delivered with success, there is several hiccups and twisted roads but on the end, when the product is finalized, a great sense of accomplishment is achieved. That's something I really enjoy, nothing better than a nice coffee and breakfast in the following day after a big project delivered.

I learned that you should set a Goal and put effort and commitment to achieve it, hard to do, easy to say, resources and time has to be allocated properly, the earlier you organize everything higher the chance of success.

Communication is key in a group, sometimes a person is so good at something and nobody knows. If you don't ask you rarely have an answer! A team where everyone truly feel comfortable to talk and work will deliver better.

That's a little bit of myself...
